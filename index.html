Key Insights & Recommendations

💎

Designer Bangles and Peacock Bracelets were the top-performing products, driving significant revenue.

📊

Product trends shifted from rings to bangles, bracelets, and necklaces, indicating evolving customer preferences.

📈

Daily budgets were strategically scaled from ₹5,000 to ₹8,000, contributing to improved performance and growth.

🎨

Catalog ads emerged as the best-performing ad format, delivering superior engagement and conversion rates.

📦

15 products went out of stock during this period, highlighting strong demand and the need for better inventory management.

🎯

Successfully tested multiple audience strategies including ad+ open audience, detailed targeting, and metro cities targeting to optimize reach.

Next Steps

1

Retarget users who visited the website, added products to cart, or reached checkout to maximize conversion opportunities.

2

Scale campaigns to ₹15,000 per day in November to capitalize on strong October performance and drive continued growth.
